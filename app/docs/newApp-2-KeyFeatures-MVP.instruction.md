    “Pocket Pet Companion” 🐾 
    A lightweight browser app where users adopt and care for a virtual pet that lives right in their tab. 
    Core concept 
    The user chooses a pet (cat, dog, maybe something quirky like a blob or alien). The pet has basic needs that change over time—even when the tab is inactive.
